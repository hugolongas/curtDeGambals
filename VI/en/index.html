Benvinguts! | ¡Bienvenidos! | Welcome!

Welcome to the sixth edition of the Curt de Gambals’ festival, organized and carried out by Ateneu Aliança Lliçà d’Amunt.

This year the festival will be immersed in the horror genre and all its sub-genres and relevant aspects along: gore, slasher, zombie, monsters, aliens, haunted houses, serial killers, survival, s B serial, Z serial, possessions, ghosts ...

The starting date of the contest and the reception of the projects is December, 1st 2013. Participants have time to present their projects until April. The delivery deadline is April, 1st 2014. The official contest presentation and the award ceremony will be the 1st of June 2014.

You can find all the information necessary to participate and the basis in the section "Join".

News

What do you need to participate?

  • An Horror Short film .
  • The maximum length of the shorts has to be 10 minutes. (Including the title and the final credits).
  • All the projects have to be submitted online on the inscription leaf /registration form you have to indicate the link.
  • The deadline to send the registration form is April 1st 2014.
You can find more information in the contest basis.

Take part!

To take part of the Curt de Gambals Contest, please send us the registration form correctly answered to: curtdegambals@lalianca.cat.
registration form

IMPORTANT: to participate the registration form has to be correctly answered and the link must be specified.

Categories

  • GENERAL:All genre of horror short films.
  • .CAT:short films in Catalan language.
  • JOVE:shorts made by young people or a young people group (up to 18 years old).

All the shorts submitted will be considered in general category.

AWARDS

  • First finalist:
    • General: awared with 300€.
    • .Cat: awared with 150€.
    • Jove: awared with 150€.
  • Audience award:
    • Most voted by the audience screenshot and award ceremony.
  • Web award:
    • Most voted on the webpage.
